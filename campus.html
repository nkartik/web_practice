Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya, Therubali is situated in the heart of the District Rayagada of Odisha State. It is situated at a distance of 25 kms. west from the district headquarters Rayagada. It is around 550 kms. away from the State capital, Bhubaneswar. The natural surrounding is full of lush green forests offering a pleasant climate. School campus itself is full of green trees, shrubs and flowers. This city is famous for Paper Mill. The Chinmayee Vidyalaya is our neighbouring institute. This Vidyalaya is newly constructed with a heart touching look and feel. The atmosphere as a whole is quite serene and conducive to the teaching and learning process.
